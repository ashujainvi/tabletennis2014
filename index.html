ABOUT THE TOURNAMENT

November 14-15
2014

The 18th annual Ajay Kumar Garg Memorial Table Tennis Tournament is to be conducted on 14th and 15th November 2014 at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in association with the Ghaziabad District Table Tennis Association. The tournament is held every year in an air-conditioned hall, contains simultaneous matches on various tables. Teams from NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, UP, and Punjab mark their participation in 10 different categories ranging from 'Under 18' to 'Over 40' for men and women separately in individual and team events. A huge amount is kept as the prize money.

Notes
  • Outstation players shall be provided free boarding & lodging (in the hostel) if required.
  • Each Institute/Organisation may send one or two teams per event. There is no limit of participants for individual events.
  • The tournament will be played on knock out basis.
  • Each team shall consist of not more than three players.
  • The tournament will be conducted as per TTFI rules.
  • There will be three players in the team and they will draw for choice of ABC & XYZ playing order & the pattern of play will be as under:
    1. A v/s X
    2. B v/s Y
    3. C v/s Z
    4. A v/s Y
    5. B v/s X
  • The decision of the tournament committee will be final in all the matters.
  • Dress regulations: All the participating players (Except Masters) are requested to wear proper kit like Half Pant, Skirt, T-Shirt etc.
